Project Title

Predicting Flight Attendant Satisfaction and Dissatisfaction

Project Overview

The objective of this project is to develop a predictive model that can assess the satisfaction and dissatisfaction levels of flight attendants based on various factors related to their work environment, personal attributes, and job experiences. By accurately predicting these levels, airlines can implement targeted strategies to enhance job satisfaction, improve employee retention, and ensure a higher quality of service for passengers.
