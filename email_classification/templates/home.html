About the Ham or Spam Detection Tool

This tool is designed to help you determine whether a given text message is considered "ham" (non-spam) or "spam". Spam messages are unsolicited, often irrelevant or inappropriate messages sent over the internet to a large number of users. Ham messages are legitimate and relevant communications.

By entering your text into the form and clicking "Check", the tool will analyze the content and provide a classification of whether it is more likely to be ham or spam. This can be useful for managing your email or messaging systems, ensuring that important communications are not lost amidst spam.
